Data Safety

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Package: com.automotive3d.app

✓ No data collected or shared

Automotive3D runs entirely on your device. No account is required, no personal data is uploaded, and no analytics or advertising SDKs are included.

Data Collection Summary

Category Collected? Shared? Purpose
Personal info (name, email, etc.) No No —
Financial info No No —
Location No No —
App activity (usage, actions) No No —
Device identifiers No No —
Build configurations Yes (local only) No Saved to your device so your build persists between sessions

Data Sharing

Automotive3D does not share any data with third parties. There are no advertising networks, analytics providers, or data brokers involved in this app.

Security Practices

All data is stored locally on your device using standard Android storage APIs (SharedPreferences / localStorage). No data is transmitted over the network. The app requests only the permissions necessary for operation.

Android Permissions

INTERNET

Required by the Capacitor app container to load the web view correctly. No external network requests are made during normal use.

ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Used to check connectivity status. No data is sent regardless of network state.

Data Deletion

Since no data is stored on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete on our end. To clear your local build configuration, uninstall the app or clear app data from Android Settings → Apps → Automotive3D → Clear Data.
