Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Automotive3D ("the App") is an interactive 3D engine bay configurator that lets you visualise real branded aftermarket parts on a virtual engine. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

No personal data is required. The App does not require an account, sign-in, or any personal information to function.

Build configurations: Your part selections and customisations are saved locally on your device using on-device storage (localStorage / SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device.

Usage analytics: The App does not collect analytics, crash reports, or any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

In-app purchases (if applicable) are processed by Google Play Billing. Google's own privacy policy governs any data collected during that transaction.

Permissions

Internet: Required for the app container to load correctly. No outbound network requests to external servers are made during normal use.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and in the app-store listings.
