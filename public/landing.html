💥 Fault Simulation Engine

See What Happens When It Breaks — Not Just When It Works

Every other engine visualiser shows you how things run. Automotive3D shows exactly what happens when they fail — in 3D, with physics-accurate explanations and real DTC codes.

Inject a fault and the affected mesh highlights with animated effects. A plain-English explanation describes the failure at the component level — what broke, why, what the symptoms are, and how to fix it.

The same fault engine covers every motor in the roster, including your custom designs.

Subsystems

7 Subsystems, fully mapped

Every part in the engine belongs to a subsystem. Tap any system to isolate and highlight it — the rest fades so you can focus.

Short Block

Block, pistons, crankshaft, oil pan, dipstick, oil filter, senders.

Heads & Valvetrain

Cylinder head, camshaft, valve covers, valvetrain — seals the bores and works the valves.

Induction

Intake manifold, carburettor, air cleaner, distributor, PCV breather, throttle linkage.

Exhaust

Exhaust manifold / tubular headers — collects spent gases from each bank's ports.

Driveline

Flywheel, bell housing, starter — where the engine meets the transmission.

Cooling & Belt Drive

Water pump, cooling fan, drive belt, thermostat — keeps everything at operating temperature.

Charging

Alternator — belt-driven generator that keeps the battery charged while running.
